NULL STATE: MISSION

Null State is an anti-fascist streetwear collective. We don't sell clothes to make money — we give them away to make noise. Our designs are weapons, our shirts are flyers, and every drop is a middle finger to systems of control. If you're tired of corporate liberalism, right-wing theocracy, or anyone profiting off oppression, you're home now.

We don’t worship brands. We don’t follow trends. We are not for sale. Each drop is hand-printed and limited, but not exclusive. We use a $1 raffle system to fund the print run. Everyone who enters is guaranteed the shirt they select — no bullshit. And five entrants each round will receive a full 5-shirt pack. Randomly. No strings. No hierarchies.

Every drop supports a rotating cause — legal aid for protestors, bail funds, immigrant defense, trans mutual aid, or wherever the boot is landing hardest that week. We publish donation receipts in a public ledger so you always know where the money went. There is no secret backend. This is DIY activism with radical transparency.

Null State is punk in execution, not just aesthetics. It’s for the disillusioned, the angry, the abandoned, the overeducated and underpaid, the burnt out, the fed up. Our only rule: make fascists afraid again.

And if you're reading this — welcome to the state with no masters.
